## Tier 1 — Draft Pack (₹4,999 / ₹2,499 launch)

### Scope (current production - PDF format)
- Auto-generated CDSCO MDR submission documents
- Content mapped to CDSCO structure with full source citations
- PDF download
- Self-serve, credit-card decision

### Scope (Sprint 2-3 upgrade per mockup)
- Section-based web view (12 sections per CDSCO MDR structure)
- All sections editable by customer
- Citation-traceable references (inline + right panel)
- "Last regenerated" timestamps
- PDF download still available
- Customer auth required (Sprint 2 Story 2.2)

### Pricing rationale

**Why dual pricing (₹4,999 list / ₹2,499 launch)?**

Approach: Launch discount mechanism, not gimmick.
- ₹2,499 is the deck-committed price (matches investor pitch)
- ₹4,999 reflects the upgraded product value (sectioned + editable + citations) when shipped
- During Sprint 2-3 transition: ₹2,499 effective price, ₹4,999 list price
- When upgraded product ships: discount lifts naturally, new customers pay ₹4,999
- Existing ₹2,499 customers keep that price OR get product upgrade free (loyalty)

### Substitutes
| Alternative | Cost | What customer gives up |
|---|---|---|
| DIY research | ₹0 + 20-40 hours founder time | Time, regulatory accuracy |
| Boutique consultant initial dossier | ₹50K-1L | Money (20-40x higher) |
| Online template marketplace | ₹2-10K | India-specific accuracy, citations |

### Justification at ₹2,499 effective
- Sub-₹2,500 is "no-brainer" tier for Indian medtech founders
- 95-98% below boutique consultant ₹50K-1L benchmark — clear disruption
- High-volume foundational tier, Tier 2 customers come from this funnel
- Margin: 95%+ (synthesis cost ~$0.13, sectioned UI infra cost minimal)

### Justification at ₹4,999 list (when discount lifts)
- Reflects sectioned + editable + citation-traceable upgrade
- Still 90% below consultant pricing
- Anchors customer perception of value

### When to revisit
- Drop discount when upgraded Draft Pack ships AND Tier 1 → Tier 2 conversion >12%
- If volume <30/month after launch, discount issue or marketing issue (not pricing)
- Don't drop below ₹2,499 — leaves money on table, undermines anchor

### Volume target
- Sprint 2: 5-10 paying customers/month at ₹2,499 launch
- Sprint 3-4: 30-60 paying customers/month
- Sprint 5+: 50-100 paying customers/month

## Tier 2 — Reviewer Concierge (₹75,000)

### Scope (per investor deck mockup page 4, Sprint 3 build)
- Everything in Tier 1
- AI + named senior regulatory reviewer concierge
- Multi-reviewer bench (2-3 named reviewers per engagement)
- Section assignment per reviewer (e.g., Sections 1-4 with anchor reviewer, 5-7 with bench reviewer)
- Engagement timeline visible to customer
- Maker-checker workflow (customer fills → expert reviews → customer responds → finalized)
- Final reviewed pack delivery (CDSCO-ready)
- 48-hour expert response SLA per iteration

### Substitutes
| Alternative | Cost | What customer gives up |
|---|---|---|
| Mid-tier regulatory consultant | ₹2-5L | Money (2.7-7x higher) |
| Big-firm regulatory shop | ₹5-15L | Money (7-20x higher), opacity |
| Tier 1 alone + uncertainty | ₹4,999 | Confidence in submission accuracy |

### Justification at ₹75,000
- 50-85% below mid-tier consultant pricing — premium-but-disruptive
- Round number psychological anchor (just below ₹1L threshold)
- Customer perceived value: "I'd pay ₹3-5L to a consultant for this"
- Margin estimate: 80% per deck (after ~₹15K reviewer fee + AI infra)

### Operations
- **Expert pool:** In-network (founder-recruited via iSPIRT/ARTPARK networks) + curated marketplace
- **Expert response SLA:** 48 hours per iteration of maker-checker workflow
- **Sprint 3 needs:** 2 anchor reviewers (named, equity), bench reviewer onboarding, vetting criteria
- **Quality control:** Expert performance tracking (response time, customer satisfaction, dispute rate)
- **Marketplace target:** 10-20 active reviewers by end of Sprint 5

### Volume target
- Sprint 3: 1-2 paying Tier 2 customers (manual onboarding, learn friction)
- Sprint 4: 3-5 paying Tier 2 customers/month
- Sprint 5+: 5-10 paying Tier 2 customers/month

### When to revisit
- If expert availability bottleneck: raise to ₹99,999
- If volume <2/month sustained: scope or marketing issue, not pricing
- If volume >10/month: raise to manage expert capacity

## Refund policy

### Tier 0 (Free) — N/A

### Tier 1 — Draft Pack (₹4,999 / ₹2,499)
- Full refund within 7 days **if PDF was not downloaded** AND sectioned view not edited
- No refund after PDF downloaded or sectioned view actively used (delivered product)
- Exception: full refund if AI generation produced clearly broken output — automatic on detection

### Tier 2 — Reviewer Concierge (₹75,000)
- Full refund within 24 hours of payment **if expert hasn't been assigned yet**
- 50% refund within 7 days if expert assigned but no review delivered
- No refund after expert delivers first review iteration

### Cross-tier rules
- **Service unavailability** beyond SLA: automatic full refund processed
- **Disputes:** customer can request founder review within 30 days; case-by-case discretion
- **Processing time:** all refunds within 5 business days via Cashfree
- **Refund policy linked from upgrade page footer per tier**

## Pricing iteration triggers

**Lift Tier 1 launch discount when:**
- Upgraded Draft Pack product (sectioned + editable + citations) shipped
- 30+ paying customers/month sustained at ₹2,499
- Tier 1 → Tier 2 conversion >12%

**Raise Tier 2 to ₹99,999 when:**
- Volume sustained >5/month for 6+ weeks
- Expert capacity becomes binding constraint
- Customer interviews confirm willingness at higher price

**Hold prices when:**
- Within ±20% of volume targets
- New tier still being optimized
- Within first 3 months of any tier launch

**Lower prices when:**
- Volume <40% of target for 6+ weeks AND customer interviews confirm price is the blocker

**Never lower Tier 1 below ₹2,499.** That's the foundational anchor and deck-committed price.
